Are you tired of manually populating questions and answers for your customer support chatbot?

Say hello to Norby! Our AI technology is designed to provide round-the-clock assistance to your clients, taking care of their needs with lightning-fast speed and accuracy.

With Norby, you can simply feed your company information and watch as Norby learns every detail of your business. But wait, there's more! Norby can even provide your customers with customized sales offers. The more information you give to Norby, the more you get in return!

Take your customer support and sales to the next level with ChatGPT chatbot Norby. Try it out today!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to start using Norby AI WordPress plugin? =

If you already have an API key, just add it to the API key field on Norby AI WordPress plugin page.

= What if I don't have any API key? =

[Please refer here to obtain a new one](https://chat.norby.io/signup).

= Where can I find the dashboard? =

The dashboard is available at [chat.norby.io](https://chat.norby.io).

= Where can I find the list of actual settings? =

Actual plugin settings are available at [Norby AI settings](https://norby-io.notion.site/How-to-Customize-the-Widget-for-Your-Needs-0ea75994da6a4fdab9ff305025dd8275).
